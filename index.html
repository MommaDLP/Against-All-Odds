About Me

My name is Miranda De La Paz, I am a mother to a three year old little girl & a wife for six years now.
I have never wanted & needed something so desperatly like I do with becoming a woman of tech.
I have worked as much as three jobs at a time just to makes ends meet while going to school.
I had my first baby girl and I could have died in recovery from complications that happen.
My hours at work have been reduced so much that most time I am scared we wont have enough to pay bills or buy groceries.
I also struggle with Hypothyroid trying to bring me down.
With all that being said no matter the struggles I have faced & the odd against me I continue to strive for better for my family.
It's now time for me to step it up & educate myself not only for me but my current family and new ones to come. I'm head strong,
driven and commited to succeed, I will become a woman in tech very soon.
